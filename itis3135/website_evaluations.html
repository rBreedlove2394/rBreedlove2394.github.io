Website Evaluations

Website 1: Team Charlotte Motorsports (BAD)

This website is a local motorcycle / atv / water sports dealership. They provide services from parts sales, full repairs, insurance clams repairs, and after market upgrades. They have a well-rounded staff that can accommodate everyone to help solve your problem.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast on nav bar between the red and white. This makes the text hard to read.
  • Repetition: Once you get into the inventory of the shop. The repetition between the photos is poor because some bikes are using a stock image instead of actual pictures.
  • Alignment: In the navigation between pages on the inventory sections, the alignment for the text is to close together making it looked smashed together.
  • Proximity: The proximity of the filter buttons is un even and mix-matched. This makes it have too much blank space.

Website 2: Amazon (GOOD)

Amazon and subsidiaries like Shopbop, and Zappos support shopping for a wide range of products without the need to travel to a physical store. And Prime shipping offers fast delivery options.

  • Contrast: The contrast for Amazon is very effective. The navigation bar is easy to read, and you can the more important advertising.
  • Repetition: Amazon Uses repeating boxes to have each section in its own field. This continues for when you see the items you are searching for.
  • Alignment: Everything in the body is center aligned for easy viewing across all different styles of view devices.
  • Proximity: Everything is evenly spaced through-out the pages giving it the look of perfect proximity
